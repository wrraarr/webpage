Collect Information Activities

  • User & field research (ethnography, interviews, surveys)
  • Literature & domain review (digital humanities)
  • Data acquisition & wrangling (data engineering, web scraping, APIs)
  • Knowledge organization (ontologies, knowledge graphs)
  • Ethics, law & policy review (IRB, privacy, accessibility)

Ask Questions Activities

  • Problem framing & research questions (HCI, design research)
  • Theories & constructs (psychology, sociology, economics)
  • Hypotheses & measures (statistics, causal inference)
  • Stakeholder & power mapping (policy, STS)

Define Problem Activities

  • Requirements engineering & use cases
  • Risk & threat modeling (security, privacy)
  • Value-sensitive & responsible design (ethics, equity)
  • Feasibility & systems architecture
  • Accessibility & inclusion (UDL, disability studies)

Imagine Activities

  • Ideation & participatory design (co-creation, storyboarding)
  • Algorithmic approaches (AI/ML, optimization, IR)
  • Data modeling (databases, visualization, knowledge representation)
  • Prototyping (hardware/embedded, robotics)
  • Speculative/critical design, futures thinking

Develop & Test Activities

  • Software engineering (frontend/backend, mobile, cloud)
  • DevOps/MLOps & reproducibility
  • Embedded/IoT systems & robotics
  • Usability testing, heuristic evals, A/B tests
  • Security, privacy & compliance testing

Improve Activities

  • Evaluation & impact assessment (stats, field trials, SNA)
  • Fairness, accountability, transparency, interpretability
  • Monitoring & reliability (SRE, telemetry)
  • Post-deployment research (ethnography, diary studies)
  • Iteration, backlog & roadmap (product/change mgmt)
